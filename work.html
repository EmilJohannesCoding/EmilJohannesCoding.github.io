My working history

I currently work part-time as a mail carrier and have been employed by the postal service since 2018 as a seasonal and part-time worker. My responsibilities include sorting mail and delivering it by car or electric scooter. This role has strengthened my ability to work independently, manage my time efficiently, and interact with a wide range of customers. Through this work, I have also developed a strong familiarity with the streets and neighborhoods of my hometown, Lahti.

In addition, I work part-time as a moving worker at Martela, where I have been employed since autumn 2025. My work involves office relocations for various companies as well as facilities belonging to the Päijät-Häme Wellbeing Services County. My responsibilities include transporting items, assembling furniture, and installing workplace equipment. This role has developed my teamwork skills and my ability to find practical and efficient solutions in different work situations.
